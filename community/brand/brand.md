# Brand

**Status:** v0 — first-pass, iterate as identity solidifies.

## Name

**Warsaw AI Community**

Short forms: `WAC`, `WarsawAI`. Avoid `WAIC` (confuses with other orgs).

Domain candidates (to acquire as commercialization path clarifies): `warsawai.community`, `warsawai.dev`, `waic.dev`.

## Positioning

> Warsaw's working community for practical AI/ML. We build, ship, and meet weekly.

Not: "meetup group." Not: "study group." Not: "AI enthusiasts." We are **builders**.

## Voice

- **Direct.** "This model is slow" beats "this model has performance considerations."
- **Specific.** Numbers, links, code snippets. No hand-waving.
- **Humble but not self-deprecating.** We don't pretend to know everything. We also don't hedge to the point of meaninglessness.
- **Fast.** Short posts. Long posts only when they earn the length.
- **Builder-to-builder.** We talk like engineers talking to engineers. Not marketers talking to leads.

## Assets

Stored in this folder as they're produced. Large binary assets (PNG, SVG, PDF) go in `community/brand/assets/`.

## Naming things

- Sub-projects: lowercase-kebab-case folder name under `projects/`. Example: `projects/gbrain`, `projects/telegram-digest`.
- ADRs: `NNNN-short-title.md`, zero-padded to 4 digits.
- Events: `YYYY-MM-DD-slug/`.
- Meeting notes: `YYYY-MM-DD.md`.

Consistency in naming is a brand decision. It makes the repo legible at a glance.
