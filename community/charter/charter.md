# Charter

## Who we are

Warsaw AI Community is a Warsaw-based community of builders, researchers, and operators who are serious about practical AI/ML. We are not a meetup group that occasionally talks about AI. We are a working community whose default mode is building.

## Why we exist

Because Warsaw has the density of talent, ambition, and proximity to European AI infrastructure to produce real products — but not enough places where people with different shapes of AI expertise meet weekly in person to pitch, challenge, and ship.

## What we do

1. **Meet offline every week** in Warsaw.
2. **Discuss the fastest-moving parts of AI/ML** — papers, models, tools, market moves.
3. **Build and ship** — member projects, OSS first, pitched to each other for feedback.
4. **Accumulate knowledge** in GBrain — a Telegram-accessible knowledge base the community owns.
5. **Incubate** — member-built projects can and should spin out, with the community as the proving ground.

## What we value

1. **Shipping over talking.** A rough v1 beats a perfect deck.
2. **Honesty over politeness.** Feedback is the product. Pretend-nice kills companies.
3. **Open by default.** Our code is OSS. Our decisions are written. Our meeting notes are public to members.
4. **Curiosity over credentialism.** The best tool won, not the most expensive degree.
5. **Proximity.** Online works, offline wins. Weekly in-person is the forcing function.

## What we don't do

- **Pretend-community.** No members-in-name-only, no bots inflating numbers.
- **Vague positivity.** "Sounds great!" without specifics is not feedback.
- **Closed-source by default.** Exceptions need a stated reason (commercial spinout, client contract, etc.).
- **Exclude on background.** Experience is welcome; gatekeeping is not.

## Horizon

- **3 months:** 50 members, GBrain v1 live, 2 member-shipped projects.
- **12 months:** 200 members, GBrain v2 with a monetization path, 12 member-shipped projects, established Warsaw AI brand.
- **24+ months:** Legal entity options exercised, first commercial product line, regional expansion considered.
