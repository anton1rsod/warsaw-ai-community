# Warsaw AI Community

> Warsaw-based community of builders, researchers, and operators focused on practical AI/ML. Weekly offline meetups + event-driven bursts. Commercial-track, OSS-first.

**Members:** 19 (as of 2026-04-24) · **Channel:** Telegram · **Status:** Early formation

---

## What we do

- **Weekly offline meetups** in Warsaw — no mandatory online sync.
- **Event-driven bursts** around major AI news, product launches, guest speakers, hackathons.
- **Async discussion** in a topic-structured Telegram channel.
- **Member-built projects** — pitched, built together, shipped OSS-first.
- **GBrain** — our upcoming Telegram-accessible knowledge base (see [`projects/gbrain/`](projects/gbrain/)).
