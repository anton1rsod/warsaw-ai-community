# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

Warsaw AI Community is a space for builders, researchers, and operators working on AI/ML. We want it to be a place where people of any background, experience level, or role can learn, ship, and be honest with each other.

## Expected behavior

- **Be kind.** Assume good intent. Disagree on substance, not identity.
- **Be specific.** "This doesn't work" is less useful than "line 12 throws because X".
- **Attack ideas, not people.** Strong technical criticism is welcome. Personal attacks are not.
- **Give credit.** Link sources. Attribute authors. Don't pass off others' work as yours.
- **Respect attention.** No spam, no unsolicited DMs, no off-topic firehose.
- **Respect opt-in.** GBrain only ingests content that is tagged or topic-consented. Don't attempt to ingest private conversations.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech on any axis (gender, race, religion, sexuality, nationality, disability, appearance, experience level).
- Doxxing or sharing others' personal information without consent.
- Sexual content, imagery, or advances in community spaces.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, meetups, or events.
- Threats, intimidation, or violence (implied or direct).
- Paid promotion disguised as genuine discussion (affiliate spam, undisclosed sponsored posts).

## Enforcement

- **First issue:** A core organizer will message you privately to explain and ask for correction.
- **Repeat / serious issue:** Temporary mute or removal from the Telegram channel and all community venues.
- **Severe issue (harassment, doxxing, threats):** Immediate removal without warning.

Core organizers make enforcement calls by lazy consensus. The founder breaks ties. Enforcement decisions for removals are logged (anonymized where needed) in [`docs/decisions/`](docs/decisions/) to maintain precedent.

## Reporting

Contact any core organizer directly on Telegram, or the founder (Anton). All reports are treated confidentially.

## Scope

This applies to all community venues — the Telegram channel, weekly meetups, events, the GitHub repo, and any other space operating under the Warsaw AI Community name.
